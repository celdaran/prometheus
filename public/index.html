About Prometheus

The Myth

Prometheus is a Titan in Greek mythology, credited with the creation of the human race and the gift of fire and metallurgy, spurring technical progress and civilization. Zeus punished him severely for this, so all in all, he seems like the perfect representation of the creative mind.

This Site

This web site offers an array of tools for the tortured, creative spirit. Whether you're a writer, a gamemaster, or just looking to kill some time, this site might just help. Anything from generating names to generating geography to generating history. It's all just a click away.
